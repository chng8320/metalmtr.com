Services

TMT Mechanics is confident of being an outstanding private enterprise specializing in the fields of lathe machining, CNC milling. In addition, we can work with a variety of materials, such as Plastic, Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Stainless Steel, Brass, Aluminum...

Our focus is to provide high quality prototype, high volume production, and quick turnaround projects for the customers. We will provide to our customers with satisfied on a wide range of services with the lowest cost.

CNC Lathe

Our CNC turning process produces custom prototypes and end-use production parts in competitive pricing at higher quantities. We use a CNC lathe with live tooling so features like axial and radial holes, flats, grooves, and slots can be machined.

CNC Mill

Our CNC milling process produces custom prototypes and end-use production parts in competitive pricing at higher quantities. We use 3-axis milling to manufacture parts from more than 30 engineering-grade plastics and metals.

CNC Programming

World class Production Support that keeps your CNC machines running 24/7. CNC programming for over 20 years our team have been at the forefront of the Precision Engineering & Manufacturing industries worldwide.

Drawing and 3D Design

Regardless of your sector and strategic targeting, our specialized R&D department help you design and develop new products and solutions and differentiate from your competition.
